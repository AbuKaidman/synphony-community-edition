SynPhony Help

Story Checking Tool

The Story Checking Tool lets you type or paste a sentence or story into the text box and check to see how much of the text can be read by a student.

Legend

The Legend indicates the colors used for marking up the sentence or story.

  • Focus words are words that are usable in a lesson because they have the letter which the lesson focusses on.
  • Good words are words that are readable because they contain only letters which have been taught in previous lessons. These words belong to the Cumulative Word List.
  • Possible words that may be readable but for one reason or another SynPhony cannot know for sure. This means that the user should check to see if the word is really readable or not. Some of the reasons why SynPhony treats these words as Possible is that it contains all the letters that have been taught but the actual word is not in the Target word list or in Good word list (or Cumulative Word List). This can happen because the word is longer and the user has not selected the appropriate Syllable length checkbox. Another reason may be that the word does not exist in SynPhony's database.
  • Sight words are words that are needed to make a sentence or story but also contain letters that have not been taught yet.
  • All other words will have the default background color. This indicates that the word is not suitable for the lesson because it contains letters that have not been taught yet.

Sight Words section

The Sight Words section can be expanded or collapsed by clicking on the text “Sight Words” Any word you type into this text box will be considered a “sight word” and, if it exists in the story you are checking, will be highlighted with this color. If you need to specify several words then separate each word with a space.

Target Words

The Target Words section contains words that are suitable for the current lesson. The list of words reflect the selections in the Options section of the Control Panel. The list of words will change depending on how many syllable and the “Show all:” checkboxes have been selected.

Story

This textbox lets you enter a sentence or a story so you can check how much of the text is readable at any particular lesson.

Pressing this button causes SynPhony to check the text that has been entered in the textbox above and add colors to words to indicate how suitable it is for a lesson.
